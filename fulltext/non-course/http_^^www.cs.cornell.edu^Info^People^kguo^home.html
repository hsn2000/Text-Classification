I am a Ph.D. student in Computer Science at Cornell, working in distributed systems. My interest is in scalable reliable multicast protocols. I am working on the Horus Project under the direction of Ken Birman, Robbert Van Renesse and Werner Vogels.

Recent Publications

  • Katherine Guo, Werner Vogels and Robbert van Renesse. Structured Virtual Synchrony: Exploring the Bounds of Virtually Synchronous Group Communication. To appear in the proceedings of the 7th ACM SIGOPS European Workshop, Connemara, Ireland, September 9-11, 1996.

  • Luis Rodrigues, Katherine Guo and Antonio Sargento, Robbert van Renesse, Brad Glade, Paulo Verisimo, Ken Birman. A Transparent Light-Weight Group Service. To appear in the proceedings of the 15th IEEE Symposium on Reliable Distributed Systems, Niagara-on-the-Lake, Canada, October 23-25, 1996. Also available as Technical Report 96-1579, Department of Computer Science, Cornell University.

  • Robbert van Renesse, Kenneth P. Birman, Brad Glade, Katherine Guo, Mark Hayden, Takako Hickey, Dalia Malki, Alex Vaysburd and Werner Vogels, Horus: A Flexible Group Communications System , CS-TR 95-1500 Department of Computer Science, Cornell University. March 23, 1995.
